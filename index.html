How Civology Works

We provide a scalable platform for researching public opinion. We'll help you develop a survey, distribute it, and analyze the results without ever leaving the platform.

01

Develop

Civology provides an advanced toolkit to assist in developing your polls, surveys, and research studies. Our software platform will guide you through the development process and includes features to accomodate even the most advanced public opinion research.

Our data scientists and public opinion researchers are available to assist you with the development process. We even offer an in-house consultancy that will entirely design your survey for you.

  • Survey design tools
  • In-House support team
02

Distribute

At Civology, we've created an innovative public opinion sampling system to distribute your survey. Traditional polling firms rely upon landline phones and suffer poor response rates which lowers their accuracy. Civology utilizes an AI driven omnichannel approach to improve accuracy and response times with your surveys.

  • Omnichannel delivery method
  • GDPR & CCPA compliant
  • Accurate and battle-tested
03

Analyze

We provide a robust toolkit to analyze the results of your survey. Our platform can assist you with performing cross-tabs on your results, visualizing the data, and generating deeper analysis.

We promote honesty and transparency with public opinion research. We'll help you understand the margin of error on your results and analyze your data responsibly.

  • Cross-tab tools
  • Data visualization studio
  • Accurate error estimation
  • Rich export features
