How Civology Works

We provide a scalable toolkit for understanding your customers, new and old. We'll help you develop and distribute a brand study and analyze the results through a single platform.

01

Develop

Civology provides an advanced toolkit to assist in developing your brand outreach, brand perception, and CX studies. Our software platform walks you through the entire process and includes features to accomodate even the most complex insights projects.

Our data scientists and customer experience experts are available to assist you with the development process. We even offer an in-house consultancy that will entirely design your survey for you.

  • Survey design tools
  • In-House support team
02

Distribute

At Civology, we've created an innovative customer sampling workflow to deploy and segment your study. Traditional approaches rely upon fixed panels and suffer poor response rates which lowers accuracy. Civology utilizes an AI driven omnichannel approach to improve accuracy and response times with your surveys.

  • Omnichannel delivery method
  • GDPR & CCPA compliant
  • Accurate and battle-tested
03

Analyze

We provide a robust toolkit to analyze the results of your survey. Our platform can assist you with performing cross-tabs on your results, visualizing the data, and generating deeper analysis.

We promote honesty and transparency with customer research. We'll help you understand the margin of error on your results and analyze your data responsibly.

  • Cross-tab tools
  • Data visualization studio
  • Accurate error estimation
  • Rich export features
